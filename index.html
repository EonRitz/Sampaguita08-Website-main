About Us



Sampaguita08 Transport Service was born from a shared vision among a group of friends who understood the true essence of reliable transportation. Named after the Sampaguita flower, a symbol of purity, simplicity, and resilience, our service is a tribute to the values that bind our team together. Our journey began with the belief that every journey should be as memorable as the destination itself.


What sets us apart is our commitment to fostering friendship with every client. We don't just pick you up and drop you off ‚Äì we become a part of your adventure. Our drivers aren't just chauffeurs; they're local guides eager to share their knowledge of the region's history, culture, and hidden gems. Whether you're a solo traveler, a family on vacation, or a group of friends seeking new horizons, you'll find not just a ride, but a friendly face ready to make your trip extraordinary.


Trust is the foundation of our service. We understand that your safety and comfort are paramount. That's why we maintain a fleet of modern, well-maintained vehicles equipped with the latest technology and safety features. Our drivers are carefully selected, not only for their driving skills but also for their integrity and dedication to customer satisfaction. When you choose Sampaguita08 Transport Service, you're choosing a partner you can trust.


From the vibrant streets of Iloilo City to the tranquil beaches of Boracay, from the historical charm of Miag-ao Church to the lush landscapes of Antique, Sampaguita08 Transport Service opens the door to the wonders of Panay Island. Our routes are carefully curated to showcase the diversity and beauty of the region, ensuring that you experience the true essence of every destination.


Whether you're planning a weekend getaway, a family reunion, a business trip, or a leisurely exploration, Sampaguita08 Transport Service is here to elevate your travel experience. Join us in creating unforgettable memories as we traverse the picturesque roads of Iloilo and beyond.
